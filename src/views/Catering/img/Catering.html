Catering

Experience the Rich Flavors of Middle Eastern Cuisine with our Catering.

Make Your Next Event Memorable

with our Specialized Catering Services

Are you planning a special event or celebration? Make it unforgettable with Ottawa Kabab’s specialized catering services. Our experienced Middle Eastern chefs specialize in creating mouth-watering dishes for Eid celebrations, birthday parties, weddings, and other special events.

For Eid celebrations, our menu features traditional dishes such as qatayef, sweet dumplings stuffed with cheese or cream and drizzled with syrup, and ma’amoul, delicious stuffed cookies made with dates or nuts. Our kabab plates, including lamb, chicken, and lamb and beef skewers, are perfect for larger events like weddings, while our hearty Syrian breakfast dishes like fatteh hummus, are ideal for smaller gatherings.

We understand that every event is unique, and we work closely with you to customize our catering menu to suit your specific needs and preferences. We offer a range of catering options, from buffet-style to plated service, and can provide all the necessary equipment, including plates, cutlery, and serving dishes.

Our catering services are available for delivery or pickup, making it convenient and hassle-free for you to enjoy our delicious and authentic Middle Eastern cuisine. Our house-made food is prepared using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients, ensuring that every dish is full of flavor.

At Ottawa Kabab, we take pride in creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for our customers, and our specialized catering services are no exception. Let us help make your next event a memorable one with our authentic and flavorful Middle Eastern cuisine.

Tell Us More About Your

Catering Requirements

Please tell us about your requirements by providing the following details:

  • Date and Time of the Event: Please let us know the date and time of your event so that we can make sure we are available to cater.
  • Type of Event: Is this a corporate event, wedding, birthday party, or another type of event?
  • Number of Guests: How many guests are you expecting? This will help us determine the amount of food and equipment needed for your event.
  • Dietary Restrictions: Do any of your guests have any dietary restrictions, such as vegetarian, vegan, or gluten-free?
  • Catering Style: Would you prefer buffet-style or plated service? Do you require servers for your event?
  • Menu Preferences: Are there any specific dishes or menu items you would like us to include in your catering order? Our menu includes a wide variety of Middle Eastern dishes, and we can customize the menu to suit your preferences.
  • Equipment: Do you require any equipment, such as plates, cutlery, or serving dishes?
